The Ethereum Computer

The Ethereum Computer brings smart contracts and blockchain technology to the entire home.

Turning Personal Assets into Income


The Ethereum Computer brokering rentals and oracles services.

The Ethereum Computer makes it possible to securely rent access to any space or compatible smart object, without intermediaries.

Anywhere where there are underused assets such as temporarily vacant apartments, there is an opportunity to make a profit using the Ethereum Computer. The Ethereum Computer enables both consumers and businesses to turn their assets into income.

Built for the Mainstream


Ethereum Computer prototype v2, as presented at MWC16.

The Ethereum Computer is a smart hub with extensive connectivity running an optimized Ethereum node and a series of decentralized applications.

Built in partnership with Samsung and Canonical, the Ethereum Computer will bring blockchain technology into every home. We expect commercially available models on Amazon.com, Newegg and other major retailers sometimes during 2017.

A Global Market


Willingness to share own assets per region. Source: Nielsen.

We believe that Airbnb's will soon become fully automated, and small business owners will prefer to rent work spaces on demand rather than commit to complex leases.

66% of the world is willing to share or rent their personal assets for financial gain, and that figure is as high as 94% in China. The Ethereum Computer is uniquely placed to address those needs, anywhere in the world.

A DAO's Flagship Product

If it accepts our Proposal for the development of the Ethereum Computer, a DAO could charge for the usage of the objects brokered by the Ethereum Computer. Determined by the DAO itself, this percentage is designed to create an incentive for the DAO's actions, maintain its economic sustainability, and reward those that helped bring it to life.

It also represents the cost of decentralization: to forgo it would prevent a user from enjoying all the advantages of decentralization, leveraging the trustless payment feature of the smart objects altogether and benefiting from the network effects. This percentage will be sent directly to the DAO, giving it the option to use the accumulated ether to support its growth, or to redistribute it to its token holders.

Entertainment & Passive Earnings

In the future, it will become a source of passive income: it's the ideal platform for Ethereum Proof-of-Stake mining, and will come preloaded with pioneering apps, for example to rent one’s hard drive space in exchange for cryptocurrencies.

Development Platform

Developers create new revenue streams for Ethereum Computer owners or a DAO by connecting new devices to the Ethereum Computer, such as wifi routers or large electrical appliances.

The Slock.it stack is completely open-source: our modular, event based framework can be extended to control any embedded devices using smart contracts on the Ethereum blockchain.

The Ethereum Computer is the ideal developement platform: we take care of installing the Ethereum framework and resolving compatibility issues between the various components of the its stack (Whisper, EVM, Web3.js, IPFS/Swarm, etc).

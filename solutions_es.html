Solutions

Slock.it is the world's trailblazer in creating blockchain infrastructures. Our technology connects the blockchain to the physical world, effectively giving connected objects an identity, the ability to receive payments and the capability to autonomously execute code.

The Economy of Things

We're building autonomous machines that help companies transition from a product to a service-led growth.

Slock.it aims to address security, identity, coordination and privacy over billions of devices. As part of the collaborative economy, it allows for any object to be rented, sold or share securely - without middlemen. And in the field of machine to machine communications, it allows for direct, cost-effective payments between objects, as well as doing away with costly centralized servers outlay. Ultimately, this provides a better experience for your customers as all transactions take place instantly and without contract.

Slock.it’s technology further reduces service providers operating costs through simplified billing, enables fraud proof accounting, and sees all corporate data silos and fragile data workflows replaced with coordination around a shared, immutable blockchain state.

For more information, please contact us.

A World of a 100 Billion Devices

We started Slock.it with realistic targets and the simplest of all use cases: the humble lock, or ‘slock’, consuming very little of the processing power available to today's SoCs.

Our technology therefore lends itself very well to deeper IoT integration, both in the commercial and industrial sectors, where we are piloting networks of autonomous devices operating as part of an Economy of Things, engaging in contracts with each other, paying each other for their services, yet never having to be centrally managed.


Slock.it brings the benefits of the Blockchain - transparency, security and auditablity - to real-world objects.

Our technology can be embedded in almost any device and the first prototypes are already in the hands of developers.

Timeline

June 2015

The Story Begins

The company's inception. Our team will spend the following months in stealth mode relentlessly developing, building and testing prototypes.

November 12th 2015

Slock.it Revealed

Slock.it is introduced at Devcon One in front of the world's leading blockchain developers. Our first product, the SHS (now renamed the 'Ethereum Computer') is announced, and demoed opening doors and powering up electrical appliances.

January 25th 2016

Snappy Ethereum Released

We release the Snappy Ethereum Framework for Ubuntu Core - allowing embedded developers to easily and securely integrate the blockchain as part of their projects.

February 11th 2016

Partnering with RWE

Our partnership with energy giant RWE is announced at Lift 2016. Together we are building Blockcharge, a revolutionary approach to Electric Vehicle charging leveraging blockchain to deliver a fantastic customer experience.

February 18th 2016

Partnering with Samsung and Canonical

A transformation set of partnerships is announced at Mobile World Congress 2016. Slock.it, Samsung and Canonical join forces to build the next generation of IoT devices based on Snappy Ubuntu Core and Samsung Artik.

March 3rd, 2016

DAO Whitepaper

The Seminal DAO Whitepaper by Christoph Jentzsch is released, defining in generic terms how to build a 100% decentralized and fair Decentralized Autonomous Organization on the blockchain. It is accompanied by complete, free, open source template code.

Q2 2016

EC Proposal to a Suitable DAO

Slock.it UG will submit a Proposal for the development of the Ethereum Computer to a suitable DAO. This Proposal will define a series of core objectives, to be reviewed at regular intervals.

Late 2016

Ethereum Computer Prototypes

Early prototypes of the Ethereum Computer are offered to proven decentralized application developers - if you have the relevant qualifications contact us to be added to the waiting list.

2017

The Year of the Ethereum Computer

Depending on the scope of the proposal to the DAO, the Ethereum Computer could make its way to the Amazon, Newegg, etc. shelves. Our aim is for it to become a mainstream piece of Consumer Electronics that will bring the blockchain to homes around the world.

2018 and beyond

New Proposals and Products

We will regularly review our client engagements and initiate the development of exciting new blockchain-based products in order to stay competitive.

Never miss a beat: 

Slock.it brings the benefits of the Blockchain - transparency, security and auditablity - to real-world objects.

Our technology can be embedded in almost any device and the first prototypes are already in the hands of developers.

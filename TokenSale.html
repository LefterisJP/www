  1. Proceed to Bity.com/a>
  2. Create a new account
  3. Login
  4. Enter your phone number and verify your account
  5. Register your Ethereum account's address
  6. Follow the instructions.
  7. Enter the amount you want to spend to buy DAO tokens. To buy more than 250 EUR, USD or CHF you will have to further verify your identity with Bity
  8. Select bank transfer
